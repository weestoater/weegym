# Quick Guide - Meal Suggestions Feature

## What's New?

Your SW Logs page now includes an intelligent **Meal Suggestions** feature that recommends what to eat based on your remaining syns!

## How It Works

### 1. Automatic Suggestions

The system automatically shows meal suggestions based on:

- ✅ How many syns you have left today
- ✅ What time of day it is (breakfast, lunch, dinner, or snack time)
- ✅ What meals you've already logged

### 2. Smart Recommendations

The suggestions adapt to your situation:

#### When You Have Plenty of Syns (70%+ remaining)

- Shows a mix of low-syn treats and free foods
- Green "lightbulb" icon
- Message: "You have plenty of syns left!"

#### Normal Day (30-70% syns used)

- Balanced suggestions of free foods and low-syn options
- Green "lightbulb" icon
- Message: "Here are some meal suggestions for you:"

#### Running Low (80%+ syns used)

- Focuses on free foods and very low-syn options
- Blue "info" icon
- Message: "You've used most of your syns. Consider these options:"
- **SP Day option** appears

#### Very Low (5 or fewer syns remaining)

- Shows ONLY Free Foods
- Yellow "warning" icon
- Message: "You're running low on syns! Here are some Free Food options:"
- **SP Day option** appears

### 3. Free Food Categories

Click the buttons to browse free foods by meal:

- **Breakfast** - Porridge, eggs, fruit salad, yogurt, omelettes
- **Lunch** - Salads, soups, jacket potatoes, grilled fish, pasta
- **Dinner** - Stir-fries, bolognese, roast chicken, chilli, curry
- **Snacks** - Fresh fruit, veg sticks, sugar-free jelly, yogurt

### 4. Speed Foods (⭐)

Items marked with a star (⭐) are **Speed Foods** - these are especially good for weight loss as they're low in energy density and high in nutrients. Examples:

- Fruits and vegetables
- Lean proteins with veggies
- Salads

### 5. SP (Speed Foods) Day Option

When you're running low on syns, the system suggests considering an **SP Day**:

**What is it?**

- Fill at least **half your plate** with Speed Foods at every meal
- You can be more flexible with syns
- Naturally boosts weight loss

**Benefits**:

- ✅ Boost your weight loss
- ✅ Don't need to count syns as strictly
- ✅ Naturally filling and nutritious

**Example SP Day**:

- Breakfast: Fruit salad with fat-free yogurt
- Lunch: Large mixed salad with grilled chicken
- Dinner: Stir-fry with lots of vegetables
- Snacks: Fresh fruit, carrot sticks, cherry tomatoes

Click "SP Day Info" to see full details!

## Accessibility Features

### For Screen Reader Users

- All suggestions have descriptive labels
- Meal cards announce: name, syn value, whether it's a Speed Food, and description
- Dynamic updates announced with "polite" priority
- Full keyboard navigation available

### For Keyboard Users

- Tab through all suggestions
- Press Enter or Space to interact with meal cards
- Use arrow keys in button groups

### For Visual Users

- Color-coded alerts (green = plenty, blue = caution, yellow = low)
- Icons support the text (never rely on color alone)
- Large, readable text
- High contrast design

## Tips

💡 **Free Foods** - These have 0 syns and can be eaten without counting!

💡 **Speed Foods** - Look for the ⭐ star icon for weight loss boosters

💡 **Low on Syns?** - Check out the SP Day option for an alternative approach

💡 **Browse by Meal** - Use the category buttons to explore free food options

## Where to Find It

The Meal Suggestions card appears on the **SW Logs** page, right after your daily summary and before the "Add Food" buttons.

It only shows when:

- ✅ You're logged in
- ✅ You have a profile with a daily syn allowance set
- ✅ You've logged at least your syn allowance info

## Need More Help?

- All suggestions are examples - you can adapt them to your preferences
- Free Foods database includes 20 common options
- Low-syn meals range from 1-7 syns
- Suggestions update in real-time as you log food
